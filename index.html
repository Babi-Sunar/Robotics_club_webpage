ABOUT US

The Robotics Club of Lumbini Engineering College (LEC) is a non-profit, student-driven community dedicated to fostering innovation, teamwork, and technological growth. Established by passionate engineering students, our club works to create a platform where ideas turn into real projects and learning transforms into hands-on experience.
